Grilled Paninis

Description

Panini sandwiches that get their distinct look from the grill marks.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon extra-light olive oil, or as needed
  • 2 slices Italian bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 3 slices deli ham, or more to taste
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice firm-ripe tomato
  • 1 thin slice onion
  • cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Drizzle olive oil on 1 side of each bread slice. Spread 1 tablespoon mayonnaise on other side of each slice. Arrange layers of ham, Cheddar cheese, tomato, and onion on top. Top with other bread slice, mayonnaise-side down.
  2. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat; spray lightly with cooking spray. Place sandwich on grill pan; weigh it down with a heavy pan. Cook until cheese is melted and grill marks appear, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
